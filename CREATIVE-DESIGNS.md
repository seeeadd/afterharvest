## 📐 TECHNICAL DETAILS

### Photo Specs (All Designs):
- **Size**: 96-110px diameter
- **Shape**: Perfect circles (border-radius: 50%)
- **Borders**: Email-safe table-based frames (no broken absolute positioning)
- **Shadows**: Layered, brand-colored (green/lavender tints)

### Logo Specs:
- **Size**: 130-140px wide
- **Opacity**: 0.80-0.85 for sophistication
- **Prominent**: Easily readable at all sizes

### Typography:
- **Name**: 24-28px, split colors or bold black
- **Title**: 9-10px, all caps, 1.5-2px tracking
- **Weights**: Mix of 300 (light), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)

### Email-Safe Elements:
✅ All accents use tables (not absolute positioning)
✅ Gradients only on backgrounds (safe in all clients)
✅ No external resources
✅ Inline CSS only
✅ Mobile responsive
