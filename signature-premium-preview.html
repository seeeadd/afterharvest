🎨 Setup Instructions

⚠️ Image Issues Detected Your profile photo URL (blob:) and Imgur logo link both failed to load. Follow the instructions below to add your images properly.

Step 1: Prepare Your Images

  1. Profile Photo: Save your image locally (JPG or PNG, 256x256px recommended, under 50KB)
  2. Afterharvest Logo: Download from Imgur or use your local file (PNG preferred, ~200px wide, under 30KB)
💡 Pro Tip: Compress First Use TinyPNG (tinypng.com) or Squoosh (squoosh.app) to compress your images before converting. Smaller files = faster loading = better deliverability.

Step 2: Convert to Base64

Use the included Python script for easiest conversion:

python3 convert-images.py your-photo.jpg afterharvest-logo.png

This creates two files:

  • profile-base64.txt - Your photo as base64
  • logo-base64.txt - Your logo as base64

Step 3: Edit Your Chosen Signature

Open one of the three signature HTML files:

  • signature-premium-editorial.html
  • signature-premium-asymmetric.html
  • signature-premium-minimal-luxury.html

Replace these placeholders:

  1. Find YOUR_PROFILE_PHOTO_BASE64_HERE and paste your profile base64
  2. Find YOUR_LOGO_BASE64_HERE and paste your logo base64
  3. Replace Your Name with your actual name
  4. Replace Your Title with your actual title
  5. Adjust the description if needed

Step 4: Test in Browser

Open your edited HTML file in Chrome or Safari to see how it looks with real images.

Step 5: Add to Gmail

Method A (Recommended):

  1. Open your signature HTML in a browser
  2. Select all (Cmd/Ctrl + A) and copy
  3. Go to Gmail Settings → Signature
  4. Create new signature and paste
  5. Save

Method B (Direct HTML):

  1. Gmail Settings → Signature → Create new
  2. Click ⋮ (three dots) → Insert HTML
  3. Paste your complete HTML code
  4. Save
💡 Pro Tip: Test Thoroughly Send test emails to yourself and check on:
  • Gmail (web and mobile)
  • Outlook (if you have business contacts)
  • Apple Mail (if relevant)

Customization Tips

Adjust Colors:

  • #2C2C2C - Primary text (rich black)
  • #6B6B6B - Secondary text (sophisticated gray)
  • #7B6B9E - Brand accent (lavender)

Resize Elements:

  • Logo: Change width value (height auto-adjusts)
  • Spacing: Modify padding and margin values

Design Philosophy

These signatures follow these premium principles:

  • Asymmetric Balance: Not centered—visually interesting
  • Editorial Typography: Strategic letter-spacing and weights
  • Sophisticated Whitespace: Generous padding with golden ratio inspiration
  • Micro-Details: Hairline rules, gradients, geometric accents
  • Intentional Color: Brand color used sparingly for maximum impact
